Archaeowhaaat?!
  • 2021 | Academic Project
  • Editorial Design

The archaeoWHAAAT?! book represents the dichotomy in the discipline of archeology. During life, the human being is faced with several movies and cartoons that create a romanticized idea of what archeology is.

Thus, the book has an informative character, as it presents the reality of this discipline. However, the reading of the texts is accompanied by cartoons and memes, to make the reading light, dynamic, and create a connection with the reader.
