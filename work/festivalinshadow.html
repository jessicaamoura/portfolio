InShadow - Lisbon ScreenDance Festival explores the representation of the body in various spaces of performance. Crossing in multiple languages, such as video, performances, installations and exhibitions.

The graphic language developed reflects the movement of the body and its shadow, unfolding throughout the festival in different materials. Such as posters, flyers, catalogs, room sheets, invitations, newsletters and publications for social media.
