Bulk
  • 2019 | Academic Project
  • Editorial Design

Bulk is a fanzine about realistic sculptures of the human body. This fanzine presents two different eras, ten sculptors and twenty sculptures so that you can learn more about each artist and each era.

The epochs mentioned are the renaissance, which takes place in the 15th century until 1618, and hyper-realism, which takes place from 1965 to the present, thus allowing a comparison to be made between these two very different epochs.
