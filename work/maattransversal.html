In the MAAT Transversal project, was developed a poster, a leaflet with the program, and a tote bag. The goal of this program is to energize the museum and the Belém area.

This program was create for all genres this will diversify throughout the month of March 2020. Thus, the public encourages to visit several times, as all projects presented, there exposed to the public for a short time.

Areas such as music, plastic arts, video, performance, and mixed media include in this program.
