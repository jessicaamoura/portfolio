Specimen Franklin Gothic
  • 2019 | Academic Project
  • Typography

The Franklin Gothic typography, created in 1902 and published by American Type Founders, was then an original typeface. The name of this font chosen in honor of Benjamin Franklin, who helped draft the Declaration of Independence, the Peace Treaty and the Constitution of the United States of America.

Thus, the specimen of the Franklin Gothic typography was created to celebrate this typographic family, in a folding poster.
