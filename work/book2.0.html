Book 2.0
  • 2021 | Academic Project
  • Collaborative project with Tânia Rodrigues
  • Editorial Design
  • AR Experience

The project approaches the theme “book” and demonstrates how it unfolds, not only through its materiality as the physical book and the digital book (e-book) but also through its entire involvement, such as files, such as audiobooks, among others.

Affection, materiality, knowledge, and emission impact are the themes interpreted in this project.

The “book” nowadays multiplies in many ways, it depends on the needs of each person and it is this vision that is intended to demonstrate.
